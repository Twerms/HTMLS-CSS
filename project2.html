What are the 7 Wonders
of the World?

What?

The 7 Wonders are places in the world that you just have to see with your own eyes!

Where?

The 7 Wonders are located all over the globe! And coming as a surprise to some, there are actually a few categories of the 7 Wonders such as the Ancient and New Wonders.

Why

Why 7? Well, who knows? Maybe all significant things just come in 7's. The planets in our Solar System, the days in a week, the colors on a rainbow, the deadly sins, Earth's continents and seas, and of course, the Wonders of the World.
